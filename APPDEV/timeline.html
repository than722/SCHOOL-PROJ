Spanish Colonization of the Philippines Timeline

Year Event
1521 Ferdinand Magellan arrives in the Philippines, claiming the islands for Spain.
1565 Establishment of the first Spanish settlement in Cebu by Miguel López de Legazpi.
1571 Foundation of Manila by López de Legazpi, making it the capital of the Spanish East Indies.
1762-1764 British occupation of Manila during the Seven Years' War.
1896-1898 Philippine Revolution against Spanish rule led by Filipino nationalist figures including José Rizal.
1898 Spain cedes the Philippines to the United States after the Spanish-American War through the Treaty of Paris.
